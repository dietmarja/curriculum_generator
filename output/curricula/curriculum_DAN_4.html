Program Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this program, graduates will be able to:

  1. Apply fundamental digital sustainability principles and methodologies in structured environments
  2. Utilize appropriate tools and technologies to support sustainability objectives in digital contexts
  3. Demonstrate awareness of ethical and environmental implications of digital systems and decisions
  4. Communicate sustainability concepts and practices effectively with diverse stakeholders
  5. Implement basic sustainability improvements in digital systems and processes
  6. Apply advanced analytics to measure, monitor and improve sustainability performance of digital systems

Curriculum Progression

This curriculum follows a progressive structure with increasing complexity:

  • Foundation Stage: Core concepts and fundamentals (early semesters)
  • Development Stage: Advanced concepts and specialized knowledge (middle semesters)
  • Application Stage: Integration, synthesis, and practical application (later semesters)

Each semester builds on the knowledge and skills from previous semesters, with prerequisites ensuring proper progression.
