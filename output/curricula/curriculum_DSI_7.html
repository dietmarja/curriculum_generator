Program Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this program, graduates will be able to:

  1. Critically evaluate advanced theories and emerging trends in digital sustainability and their implications for organizations
  2. Design and lead complex sustainable digital transformation initiatives aligned with organizational strategy
  3. Synthesize knowledge from multiple disciplines to develop innovative sustainable solutions to digital challenges
  4. Evaluate and optimize sustainability performance across digital systems and organizational processes
  5. Demonstrate strategic leadership in promoting sustainable digital practices and embedding them in organizational culture
  6. Lead multi-stakeholder initiatives to address systemic sustainability challenges through digital innovation

  • Sector-specific Green IT Applications (5 ECTS): Specialized green IT approaches for different industry sectors

Curriculum Progression

This curriculum follows a progressive structure with increasing complexity:

  • Foundation Stage: Core concepts and fundamentals (early semesters)
  • Development Stage: Advanced concepts and specialized knowledge (middle semesters)
  • Application Stage: Integration, synthesis, and practical application (later semesters)

Each semester builds on the knowledge and skills from previous semesters, with prerequisites ensuring proper progression.
