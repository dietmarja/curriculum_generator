Program Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this program, graduates will be able to:

  1. Apply advanced principles of digital sustainability to various professional contexts
  2. Evaluate sustainability impacts of digital technologies using appropriate frameworks
  3. Implement sustainability-focused improvements in digital systems and workflows
  4. Communicate sustainability considerations to diverse stakeholders
  5. Collaborate effectively in teams addressing digital sustainability challenges
