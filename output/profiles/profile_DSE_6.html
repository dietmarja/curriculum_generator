Educational Profile: Data Engineer (EQF 6)

Implements and optimizes digital infrastructure with minimal environmental impact

Profile Overview

Role

EQF Level

6

Main Area

Data

Thematic Area

Sustainability

Program Duration

6 semesters

Target ECTS

180 ECTS

Learning Outcomes

Graduates with this educational profile will be able to:

  • Evaluate technical implementation for sustainable business practices
  • Assess energy efficiency in circular economy applications
  • Compare infrastructure optimization to support sustainable development goals
  • Solve resource management in organizational sustainability contexts
  • Develop technical implementation for sustainable business practices
  • Solve systems design for sustainable business practices
  • Apply integrated knowledge of energy efficiency, infrastructure optimization to solve complex sustainability challenges
