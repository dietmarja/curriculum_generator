Educational Profile: Digital Sustainability Manager (EQF 6)

Implements and manages digital sustainability initiatives

Profile Overview

Role

Digital Sustainability Manager (DSM)

EQF Level

6

Main Area

Management

Thematic Area

Sustainability

Program Duration

6 semesters

Target ECTS

180 ECTS

Learning Outcomes

Graduates with this educational profile will be able to:

  • Develop program management to support sustainable development goals
  • Optimize resource allocation in organizational sustainability contexts
  • Implement program management in digital transformation for sustainability
  • Develop program management in circular economy applications
  • Examine implementation oversight for addressing environmental challenges
  • Optimize organizational change to enhance environmental performance
  • Evaluate sustainability strategy to enhance environmental performance
  • Evaluate organizational change for addressing environmental challenges
  • Apply integrated knowledge of resource allocation, organizational change to solve complex sustainability challenges

Skills

  • green IT budget allocation
  • ESG performance monitoring
  • sustainability program management
  • sustainability team leadership
  • sustainable procurement
  • stakeholder communication
  • carbon footprint calculation
  • environmental impact assessment

Competencies

  • Capability in developing sustainable digital program management
  • Capability in developing ESG performance monitoring and reporting
  • Capability in developing sustainable digital operations oversight
  • Capability in developing green IT resource management
  • Capability in developing sustainability team coordination
  • Proficiency in developing digital tools for sustainability assessment
  • Ability to analyze and interpret sustainability data for developing decision-making
  • Capacity to communicate sustainability concepts to diverse stakeholders
  • Skill in developing sustainable development frameworks in digital contexts
