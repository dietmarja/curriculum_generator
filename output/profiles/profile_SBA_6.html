Educational Profile: Sustainability Business Analyst (EQF 6)

Analyzes business processes and sustainability performance metrics

Profile Overview

Role

Sustainability Business Analyst (SBA)

EQF Level

6

Main Area

Analysis

Thematic Area

Sustainability

Program Duration

6 semesters

Target ECTS

180 ECTS

Learning Outcomes

Graduates with this educational profile will be able to:

  • Assess impact assessment to support sustainable development goals
  • Implement sustainable business models to enhance environmental performance
  • Assess data-driven decision making in digital transformation for sustainability
  • Examine data-driven decision making in digital transformation for sustainability
  • Compare sustainable business models to support sustainable development goals
  • Implement data-driven decision making in organizational sustainability contexts
  • Examine requirements analysis for sustainable business practices
  • Apply integrated knowledge of sustainable business models, impact assessment to solve complex sustainability challenges
