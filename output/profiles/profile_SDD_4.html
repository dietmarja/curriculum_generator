Educational Profile: Software Developer for Sustainability (EQF 4)

Implements sustainable coding practices and develops eco-efficient applications

Profile Overview

Role

Software Developer for Sustainability (SDD)

EQF Level

4

Main Area

Software Development

Thematic Area

Sustainability

Program Duration

2 semesters

Target ECTS

60 ECTS

Learning Outcomes

Graduates with this educational profile will be able to:

  • Demonstrate sustainable design for addressing environmental challenges
  • Identify energy-efficient interfaces in organizational sustainability contexts
  • Identify accessible interfaces in organizational sustainability contexts
  • Perform sustainable design to enhance environmental performance
  • Identify ethical design principles to enhance environmental performance
  • Apply energy-efficient interfaces for addressing environmental challenges
  • Implement sustainable design for addressing environmental challenges
  • Operate ethical design principles for addressing environmental challenges
  • Apply integrated knowledge of accessible interfaces, ethical design principles to solve complex sustainability challenges

Skills

  • sustainable design principles
  • accessibility implementation
  • design systems optimization
  • energy-efficient UI/UX design
  • digital sustainability assessment
  • lifecycle thinking application

Competencies

  • Capability in applying energy-efficient digital design
  • Capability in applying sustainable user experience development
  • Capability in applying accessibility implementation
  • Capability in applying sustainable design systems management
  • Capability in applying ethical design principles application
  • Proficiency in applying digital tools for sustainability assessment
  • Ability to analyze and interpret sustainability data for applying decision-making
  • Capacity to communicate sustainability concepts to diverse stakeholders
  • Skill in applying sustainable development frameworks in digital contexts

Assessment Methods

  • Practical Assignments
  • Portfolio
  • Skills Demonstrations

Career Pathways

  • Sustainable Design Systems Architect
  • Ethical Design Director
  • Chief Sustainable Design Officer
  • Sustainable Design Consultant
  • ESG Integration Specialist
  • Sustainability Educator/Trainer
