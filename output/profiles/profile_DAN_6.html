Educational Profile: Data Analyst (EQF 6)

Analyzes and visualizes data to drive sustainability insights and reporting

Profile Overview

Role

Data Analyst (DAN)

EQF Level

6

Main Area

Data

Thematic Area

Sustainability

Program Duration

6 semesters

Target ECTS

180 ECTS

Learning Outcomes

Graduates with this educational profile will be able to:

  • Solve reporting to support sustainable development goals
  • Analyze data-driven insights in digital transformation for sustainability
  • Compare metrics development for sustainable business practices
  • Compare reporting for sustainable business practices
  • Develop metrics development for sustainable business practices
  • Evaluate reporting in organizational sustainability contexts
  • Apply integrated knowledge of data analysis, metrics development to solve complex sustainability challenges

Skills

  • analytics tool development
  • impact dashboard creation
  • sustainability reporting automation
  • ESG metrics development
  • sustainability data modeling
  • carbon footprint calculation
  • circular economy principles application
  • sustainability frameworks application

Competencies

  • Capability in developing sustainability data modeling and analysis
  • Capability in developing ESG metrics framework development
  • Capability in developing data-driven sustainability reporting
  • Capability in developing analytical dashboard development
  • Capability in developing impact assessment methodologies
  • Proficiency in developing digital tools for sustainability assessment
  • Ability to analyze and interpret sustainability data for developing decision-making
  • Capacity to communicate sustainability concepts to diverse stakeholders
  • Skill in developing sustainable development frameworks in digital contexts
