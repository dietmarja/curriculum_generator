Educational Profile: Sustainability Technical Specialist (EQF 4)

Provides specialized technical support for sustainability tools and platforms

Profile Overview

Role

Sustainability Technical Specialist (STS)

EQF Level

4

Main Area

Technical Implementation

Thematic Area

Sustainability

Program Duration

2 semesters

Target ECTS

60 ECTS

Learning Outcomes

Graduates with this educational profile will be able to:

  • Operate environmental impact reduction for addressing environmental challenges
  • Demonstrate technology assessment for sustainable business practices
  • Identify sustainable technologies in organizational sustainability contexts
  • Explain environmental impact reduction to enhance environmental performance
  • Demonstrate technology assessment in digital transformation for sustainability
  • Explain green IT to support sustainable development goals
  • Apply integrated knowledge of sustainable technologies, green IT to solve complex sustainability challenges

Skills

  • e-waste reduction strategies
  • green technology assessment
  • circular IT implementation
  • sustainable IT solutions design
  • stakeholder communication
  • sustainable development goals alignment

Competencies

  • Capability in applying green technology evaluation
  • Capability in applying sustainable IT solutions design
  • Capability in applying environmental impact assessment of technology
  • Capability in applying circular technology implementation
  • Capability in applying technology sustainability consulting
  • Proficiency in applying digital tools for sustainability assessment
  • Ability to analyze and interpret sustainability data for applying decision-making
  • Capacity to communicate sustainability concepts to diverse stakeholders
  • Skill in applying sustainable development frameworks in digital contexts

Assessment Methods

  • Practical Assignments
  • Portfolio
  • Skills Demonstrations

Career Pathways

  • Circular IT Implementation Lead
  • Green Technology Assessment Lead
  • Chief Green Technology Officer
  • Sustainable Technology Research Lead
  • ESG Integration Specialist
  • Digital Sustainability Researcher
